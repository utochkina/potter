Shortly about me J.K. Rowling:

J.K. Kathleen Rowling is a famous British writer, author of the Harry Potter series of novels, Knight Commander of the Legion of Honor. Born July 31, 1965 in the town of Yate (Gloucestershire). In addition to Joan, the family also had a younger sister, Dianne. When the future writer was 4 years old, her family moved to Winterbourne, where she and her sister often played with children named Potter. Joan attended St. Michael's Elementary School, founded 200 years ago by British philanthropist William Wilberforce. It is believed that the headmaster of this school, Alfred Dunn, became the prototype of the headmaster of the school where Harry Potter studied.
